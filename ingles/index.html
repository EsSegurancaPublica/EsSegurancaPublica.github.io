WHAT'S PUBLIC SAFETY?

Public safety implies that citizens of the same region can live in harmony, where each one respects the individual rights of the other. The State is the guarantor of public security and the maximum responsible in the time to avoid the alterations of the social order

In this sense, public security is a service that must be universal (it must cover all people) to protect the physical integrity of citizens and their assets. For this, there are security forces (such as the police), which work in conjunction with the judiciary.

Public security forces must prevent and prosecute potential offenses as they occur. It is also the function of the security forces to prosecute the offenders and hand them over to justice, who are responsible for establishing the corresponding punishments in accordance with the law.
