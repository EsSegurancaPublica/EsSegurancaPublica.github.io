1. About us

This site was made by students of the computer science course for internet, from the federal institute of Espirito Santo (IFES) that came about through a work proposed by our web programming teachers, in which there was a relation with another work of history where we could Choose certain themes that should have something involving our state. And through this came the idea of ​​creating such a site, especially for the fact that happened in our state, which was the police strike that happened at the beginning of this year (2017), we felt it on our skin and so we would like to show it to others Areas of Brazil and pro Mundo. But to make a page on the internet, we would like to do something more comprehensive so our teacher gave us the idea to think of a more comprehensive topic as public security, and so we did the site, if you want to contact to report some error or to give us new ideas for the site is just click on contacts there above, our chats for access.
