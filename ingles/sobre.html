Public Security

By the Constitution, public safety is the responsibility of the federal and state governments. The Ministry of Justice is the highest body responsible for the elaboration and implementation of strategies to combat violence and crime.  

Responds to this ministry, for example, the Federal Police, which investigates national crimes, such as drug trafficking, corruption and smuggling. The states and the Federal District are responsible for the execution of security actions, in charge of the Military (repression and prevention) and Civil (research) police.

Municipalities have no responsibility for public security, But some cities instituted the Municipal Civil Guard to assist the other police in maintaining public order. In this institutional setting, the bachelor in Public Safety is an expert in the sector, prepared to elaborate programs and policies.

He understands in depth the country's security problems, considering the role of the State and its agents, Taking into account the interests of society - issues such as human rights and citizenship. Its role is to analyze the evolution of violence rates, as well as issues related to environmental security and civil defense.

It is possible to enter the area with the training of technologist. In this case, the performance of the professional is focused on the practical activities of the career.
